Amazon Portability API and GDPR Compliance

Specific Notice regarding Amazon Data: Our Application utilizes the Amazon Portability API to allow You to view a summary of your shopping history ("Wrapped").

Local Processing Only: We value your privacy above all. The data retrieved via the Amazon Portability API (including Order History and Item details) is processed exclusively on Your local device. This data is NOT transmitted to Our servers, stored in Our cloud, or shared with third parties by Us. It remains strictly on your phone.

For EU Users (GDPR):

  • Data Controller: Your Tomo LLC acts as the controller of the App, but You retain control of the data on Your device.
  • Legal Basis: We process this data based on Contractual Necessity (to provide the "Wrapped" service you requested) and Consent (when you log in via Amazon).
  • Data Minimization: We only access the specific data points required to generate your summary.

Collecting and Using Your Personal Data

Types of Data Collected

When you use the "Login with Amazon" feature, the Application retrieves specific shopping history data to generate your summary. This data is stored and processed locally on your Device. It is not collected by the Company's servers.

Usage Data

Usage Data is collected automatically when using the Service. This may include Your Device's IP address, device type, operating system, and other diagnostic data. This data is used solely for app stability and performance analytics.

Use of Your Personal Data

The Company may use Personal Data for the following purposes:

  • To provide and maintain our Service: Including generating your shopping summary locally on your device.
  • To manage Your requests: To attend and manage Your requests to Us.
  • For other purposes: We may use anonymous Usage Data for data analysis, identifying usage trends, and determining the effectiveness of our campaigns.

Retention of Your Personal Data

Amazon Data: Because Amazon shopping data is stored locally on Your Device, We do not retain it on our servers. The data exists on Your device only for as long as You have the Application installed or until You clear the Application data.

Usage Data: The Company will retain anonymous Usage Data for internal analysis purposes to improve the Application.

Transfer of Your Personal Data

Amazon Shopping Data is NOT transferred to the Company. It is transferred from Amazon directly to Your Device via the API. Usage Data (metadata) may be processed at the Company's operating offices.

How to Delete Amazon Data: Since this data is stored locally, You can delete it entirely by simply uninstalling the Application or clearing the App's storage in your phone settings. We do not hold a copy of this data on our servers.
